# Business Value of Customer Segments

Knowing if a customer is a **Champion** or **Lost** allows you to spend your marketing budget smarter.

## 1. Champions (The "VIPs")
*   **Who they are:** Your best customers. They buy often and spend a lot.
*   **Why they are useful:** They generate most of your revenue (Pareto Principle: 20% of customers = 80% of revenue).
*   **Action Strategy:** **Retention & Reward**
    *   ✅ **Do:** Give them early access to sales, exclusive products, or a dedicated support line.
    *   ✅ **Do:** Ask for reviews or referrals (they love you already).
    *   ❌ **Don't:** Spam them with discounts (they will buy anyway).

## 2. Lost (The "Ghosts")
*   **Who they are:** Customers who haven't bought in a long time (e.g., >1 year).
*   **Why they are useful:** They are a "leak" in your business. Identifying them helps you stop wasting money or try to fix it.
*   **Action Strategy:** **Win-Back or Cut Losses**
    *   ✅ **Do:** Send a "We Miss You" email with a **huge discount** to tempt them back.
    *   ✅ **Do:** Ask for feedback: "Why did you leave?"
    *   ❌ **Don't:** Keep sending regular newsletters (they ignore them and it hurts your email reputation).

## 3. At Risk (The "Warning Signs")
*   **Who they are:** Regular customers who haven't bought recently.
*   **Why they are useful:** You can **save** them before they become "Lost".
*   **Action Strategy:** **Re-Engagement**
    *   ✅ **Do:** Remind them of what they bought before.
    *   ✅ **Do:** Show them new arrivals similar to their past purchases.

## Summary Table

| Segment | Goal | Recommended Action |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Champion** | **Keep them Happy** | VIP Perks, Loyalty Points, No Discounts needed |
| **New Customer** | **Make them Loyal** | Welcome Emails, "How to Use" guides, 2nd Purchase Discount |
| **At Risk** | **Wake them Up** | "Come Back" offers, Product Recommendations |
| **Lost** | **Win Back or Ignore** | Aggressive Discount (one-off) or Stop Marketing |
